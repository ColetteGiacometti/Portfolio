About Me

I grew up in France, surrounded by paint and brushes. After practicing law for more than 7 years, my creative side needed release.
Although creativity drew me to the UX/UI path, I quickly fell in love with the design process as a whole.
Using the skills gained as a lawyer, I place users at the very center of every step, going deep to understand their needs and struggles.
This empathy fuels me to find judicious and mindful solutions.
